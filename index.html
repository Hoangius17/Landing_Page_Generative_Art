About project

This is a generative art project created in p5.js to raise awareness among people about the danger of LIFE BELOW WATER - Goal 14 from the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs). We aim to create 4 websites that combine art and technology to contain generative art with strong calls to action so as to engage audiences and also to encourage them to take a meaningful action together.


Furthermore, our mission is highlighting and criticizing the devastating impact of industrialization on marine ecosystems.

Status of water pollution

Water pollution currently stands as a critical global emergency, characterized by a shift from visible waste to pervasive, invisible threats. While industrial dumping and untreated sewage remain severe issues in developing nations, ecosystems worldwide are now increasingly choked by microplastics and "forever chemicals" (PFAS) that persist for centuries. Additionally, agricultural runoff laden with fertilizers is causing massive "dead zones" in oceans and toxic algae blooms in freshwater sources. Despite growing regulatory efforts and new cleaning technologies, the rate of contamination often outpaces mitigation, threatening global biodiversity and limiting access to safe drinking water for millions.
